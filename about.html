About Us

Quinten Vanagt

Quinten Vanagt (he/him) I’m a Brussels-based media artist with a background from KASK, holding a bachelor in Media Art and a master in Fine Arts. My work explores emerging technologies such as Gen-AI, 3D animation, Virtual Reality, and Game Development as tools to critique data collection practices and surveillance capitalism. Together with César, I have collaborated on theatre performances and video installations. Additionally, I co-run NICE ARTS, a creative studio in Molenbeek specializing in visual campaigns and creative projects for Brussels-based art institutions and commercial clients.

Cesar Vromman

Cesar (he/him), has been immersed in the Drama program at KASK in Ghent, where I developed my skills as a performer and, more importantly, as a creator. My passion lies in storytelling through various media, with a focus on the merging of video/digital art and theatre/ performance art. I hold a master's degree in Theatre from KASK & Conservatorium and an additional year in Philosophy (ethics) from KUL. Next to art I'm also a political militant and active in the social field of Brussels, the city I was born and raised in.
